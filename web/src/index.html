About Scary Monkey Incorporated.

Welcome to Scary Monkey Incorporated, the culmination of a passion for digital art that began over 20 years ago. My journey into 3D modeling started in 2008 with Blender, and I've spent more than a decade honing my craft and bringing complex ideas to life.
 
My expertise isn't limited to a single discipline; I have a comprehensive skillset that includes graphic design, web development, and game development, giving me a holistic understanding of the entire creative pipeline. I am currently furthering my technical foundation by pursuing a degree in Computer Science. This unique blend of artistic vision and software development knowledge allows me to tackle creative challenges from every angle.

Post Apocalypse Asset Pack (2025)

A collection of objects created for an asset pack with a "Post Apocalypse" theme. All assets were created in Blender, and PBR materials were all created using Photoshop, Materialize, and Substance Sampler. Scenes shown above were rendered with Cycles.

Low Poly Collection (2025)

A collection of low-poly objects created for use in extremely lightweight projects, utilizing PBR materials to aid in creating a realistic looks. Models created in Blender 4.5, materials created in Photoshop and Materialize.

PBR Material Study (2025)

A collection of high-quality 4K PBR materials created from real-world photography. Photographs were shot primarily on the West Coast of the USA, and made into seamless textures with Photoshop, then turned into PBR texture maps with either Materialize or Substance Sampler. Screenshots are from Blender 4.5 using Cycles.

First Person Puzzle Concept (2025)

A prototype of a physics-based first-person game, with complex physics interactions and a robust character controller. Created with Unity 6.

The Grand Archive of Whispers (2025)

An early prototype of a 2.5D mystery game featuring Arthur as the protagonist, a young student who finds himself involved in a complex time-loop, the player is tasked with discovering the truth about the archives little by little as the loop repeats throughout. The game features 2D sprites in a 3D environment, creating a unique blend of high fidelity 3D graphics and sprites. Models and textures created with Blender, Game created with Unity.

Tarot 2D (2024)

A concept for a 2D RPG based around tarot cards. Features a working inventory system and player movement, as well as saving and loading basic data. Created in Unity. (Assets in prototype made by Pixel-Boy and AAA).
